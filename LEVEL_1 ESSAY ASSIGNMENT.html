My first day in a tech school

On a faithful morning, i woke up to the sound of prayer from a loud speaker coming from a mosque behind my house calling people for prayer,i was forced to wake up around 5 am as that was not part of my plan,my plan was to wake up by 6 am,because i will be having my first lecture by 8 am,fortunately to my favour, i was able to cook,bathe,dressed up,and eat after without rush. I left home around 7:30 am and got to school by 8 am,as soon as i entered into the school compound,i felt different because i was about to start an adventure, a new one to my system, there was a little tension at first due to my zero knowledge about tech prior to my first class,meeting with strangers to becoming close with them is also a challenge. We all sat down waiting for our tutor, here comes the most interesting part which is lecture time, i was hoping to see a very big,tall person to come in as our tutor, but to my surprise reverse was the case, a young, cute,well dressed man walked in and introduced himself with a bright smile all over his face. Believe me, stature did not do justice to what this intelligent young man caries, so brilliant and i was jealous of him at some point. He gave us lecture which lasted for 2 hours nonstop, it was a very interesting and an interactive class, he asked us some questions which i could not give an answers to,he gave us words of encouragement and i quote say whatever answers comes first to your head,you will always be right, he stated further and said there is no wrong answer in my class,as long as you have an idea That word really boosted my morale and i told myself that I CAN DO IT, he gave us an assignment at the end of the class. The young guru i was talking about since goes by the name Dr. Anonymous in case you meet with him someday. That was the beautiful experience i had on my first day in tech school.
