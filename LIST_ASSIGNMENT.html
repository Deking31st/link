How to make pounded yam with Egusi
After peeling and slicing the yam, wash it in a clean water,then pour it inside pot,and put it on fire for it to get cooked. Pound it with mortal and pestle when it get soften to your preferred texture. To make Egusi,slice your onion,put a pot on fire with some palm oil,pour the sliced onions inside,mix the mellon with water then our it inside the hot palm oil,add your seasoning,Maggi and salt,after a while,you can add the fish and ponmo,allow it to boil and our the Ugwu leaf after,cover it for some minutes for it to cooked,your pounded yam with Egusi is ready.
